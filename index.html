North Star Township

St. Louis County, Minnesota

Township Board

The board consists of three elected supervisors. It holds monthly public meetings and elections are held during the annual meeting in the spring.

Fire/EMS

The North Star Volunteer Fire Department provides emergency medical and fire/rescue services to the local area.

Recycling Center

The recycling center is located next to the town hall and accepts recycling materials only during open business hours.
